Welcome to My Website

On this website you will see me rate all the countries I have visited throughout my life.

These are the countries I have visited and stayed in for a minimum of 2 Days

Italy:

I have stayed in Italy for about 10 days and traveled to most of the major cities, including Rome, Milan, Naples, the Amalfi Coast, and Venice.

These 2 pictures were taken from the same spot just turned 180 degrees which I thought was very cool.

Rating for Italy:

I would probably rank Italy in my top 3 countries I have visited. The views were amazing and all of the places that I visited were very cool, even if I didn't get to visit the Colosseum. The food, of course, was amazing, which is kind of what you expect from a country like Italy. The only reason it is not the first in my rankings is because even though everything was good, I still think that one other country, which I will reveal later, had everything Italy had and better, and created more memories for me. I would give it an 8/10 and would absolutely recommend it to someone.
